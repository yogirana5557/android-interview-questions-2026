# Question 243: What is Mockito/MockK? Why are they used?

**Category:** Testing & Automation  
**Difficulty:** Mid

---

### Answer

They are mocking frameworks used to create fake (mock) implementations of classes. This allows you to test a target class in isolation by specifying how its dependencies should behave, without executing real database queries or network requests.
