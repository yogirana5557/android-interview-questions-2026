# Question 61: What is Android Jetpack?

**Category:** Jetpack Components & Architecture  
**Difficulty:** Junior

---

### Answer

Android Jetpack is a suite of libraries, tools, and architectural guidance provided by Google to help developers write high-quality apps easier. It simplifies complex tasks, reduces boilerplate code, and promotes a standard architecture (such as MVVM) that is robust, testable, and backward-compatible.
